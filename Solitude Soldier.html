Solitude Soldier

This is a Roamer subclass, ment for those who wish to sneak around and deal damage from the flank.

The Black Box allows you to gain up to 20 HP per shot, 1 HP per 5-6 damage.

The Choncheror allows you to gain 1-4 HP RPS in case you take damage.

In case of taking WAY too much damage, switching to the Escape Plan you can book it out of there and let the Conch do its work.
